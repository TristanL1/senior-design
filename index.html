Welcome to Santa Clara University's AR Interactive Tour Web Application!

A little bit about our application:

  • Why did we choose AR? We believe that AR will give our guests and tourists a fun and interactive way to learn about our campus! Our application allows users to customize their tour experience by letting them to choose when and where they want to visit.
  • To use our application, scan a landmark on SCU's campus (to navigate to a landmark, see our interactive map). When a valid landmark is scanned, you can choose to read more about the history of the building, what it is currently used for, and famous alumni!
  • Some buildings have fun mini quizzes to test your knowledge of our university!
  • Note: Our application will require a stable internet/data connection, in addition to access to your camera and location
