SCDI

Background Information

Made possible by a $100 million gift from John A. '60 and Susan Sobrato, the Sobrato Campus for Discovery and Innovation, or SCDI, represents Santa Clara's commitment to be a world-class leader in STEM education. SCDI is a $300 million complex outfitted with cutting-edge technology that demonstrates Santa Clara's dedication to become a world-class leader in STEM education. For the School of Engineering and the College of Arts and Sciences, the Sobrato Campus for Discovery and Innovation (SCDI) will house classrooms, labs, research, study, and office space. This complex will bring together multiple departments to create a collaborative learning environment where students and faculty can benefit from the expertise of their peers.
